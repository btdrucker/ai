💻 On your new laptop (download + decrypt)
5. Decrypt the archive
gpg --decrypt ssh_keys.tar.gpg > ssh_keys.tar

￼
6. Extract into .ssh
mkdir -p ~/.ssh
tar -xvf ssh_keys.tar -C ~/.ssh

￼
7. Fix permissions
chmod 700 ~/.ssh
chmod 600 ~/.ssh/id_rsa
chmod 644 ~/.ssh/id_rsa.pub

￼
8. (Optional) load key
ssh-add ~/.ssh/id_rsa
